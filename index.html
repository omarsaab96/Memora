About Us

Based in Lebanon, Memora Aligners is a next-generation clear aligner solution designed with shape memory technology, to optimize teeth movement control and treatment predictability. Our aligners are directly 3D-printed with the advanced Graphy Tera Harz TC-85 resin, using shape driven biomechanics.

The Tera Harz TC-85 is a photopolymer material designed to overcome the current limitations of sheet-type thermoformed aligners. It has biocompatible properties and makes orthodontic treatment more efficient.

Why Memora?

  • At oral temperature, the shape memory aligner returns to its original shape and completely grabs the tooth exerting a consistent light force to achieve and effective movement
  • The uniform thickness combined with the stable mechanical properties (shape memory) ensure more accurate tooth movement
  • Biocompatible material
  • Fewer attachments needed and a shorter treatment time
  • Customized design and thickness for better fit and more effective treatment
  • Direct printing of aligners eliminates the need for models, reducing waste and supporting eco-friendly production practices
  • Faster workflow from designing to finished aligner
